About the game
The game follows a little pig called Mersu who doesn't know what to do with his life and is offten bored. Until he is captured with his family and then taken to a castle to be prepared for a feast. He manages to escape but then finds himself lost in the castle. Hungry and alone he starts exploring it's dark rooms.

In this castle Mersu will come to find that every floor is unique and the creatures living in the rooms there are unique to the floor as well. There are the ones full of sins, ones full of love, ones full of masks and adictions as well as hate.

To get from one room to the next one Mersu needs keys to unlock the doors. To get the keys he will have to trade or do tasks for the creatures he meets, or collect them from the ones he kills.
